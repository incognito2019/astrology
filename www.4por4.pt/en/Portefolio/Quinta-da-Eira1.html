Quinta da Eira

WEBSITE with RESPONSIVE DESIGN

Explore

LOVELY AND UNFORGETTABLE SPACE

Quinta da Eira, started as a simple rural property, cultivated by a dedicated housekeeper named Barbosa, who one day, due to age and tiredness, decided to reform.

After a few years of uncertainty and relative abandonment, a group of investors decided to restore the house, making it a space with multiple values and activities.

Results

We quickly fall in love with this property where time runs slowly and priorities are measured by a different compass, making life easier thanks to the friendliness and availability of its inhabitants. It were those factors that helped us to develop an attractive website, where interactivity, usability and navigability were always present.
