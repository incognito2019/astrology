FRUUT

Website and Online Store w/ responsive design

Explore

the healthy snacks brand

Fruut, is a Portuguese brand that provides dehydrated apple slices, wich doesn´t contain added sugar or other additives.

The snacks provides a funny, alternative and healthy food, that competes with salty and sugary products.

Results

The goal for this website has been achieved by the development of a dynamic, interactive and above all impressive  layout. We decided to place and arrange some elements related to the graphic line that already exists, and create an excellent navigation and usability without neglecting the latest techniques of "responsive design".
