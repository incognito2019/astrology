Your ice cream store in Rua das Flores

Ice Flavors is a franchisee of the brand Farggi, that sells ice creams, crepes, waffles, pastry products and drinks.

It´s located in Rua das Flores, a street in the parishes of Sé and Vitória, which is now one of the busiest and most visited areas of Oporto.

Results

The design developed and applied in several supports, transmits impact, innovation and creativity.

 

In general, in all supports developed, we intend to convey the quality of the products and at the same time give a quick and simple way for customers to choose their ice cream.
