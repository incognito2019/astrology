YOUR FUTURE RESIDENCE IN PORTUGAL

StudentVille presents the first (and only) purpose-built student residence in Lisbon, which has been carefully designed to be the best.

Unlike most other residences, none of their rooms are shared, and all have a double bed and en-suite bathrooms.

Results

StudentVille believes that a student residence is way more than just a place to sleep, but rather a community you should be able to enjoy.

 

So, we were challenged to develop an online platform that would bring together the several residences they own and allow any visitor to book a room for a short period of time or for a semester.
