THE AGRICULTURE’S OPERATING SYSTEM

WiseCrop is a decision support platform that helps producers and farmers to make the best decisions every day of their campaign and harvest.

It consists of an integrated system, based on a wireless monitoring network that collects information on an ongoing basis.

Results

By accessing the platform, the producer can monitor his production, do (almost) all tasks via smartphone or quickly find products and information on the sector.


So, we developed a design that fits with this platform, that is attractive, equipped with a set of innovative, unique and distinct features (such as animated gifs) and which allows the visitor an excellent user experience.
