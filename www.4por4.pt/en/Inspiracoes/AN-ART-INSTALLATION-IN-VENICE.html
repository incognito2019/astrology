AN ART INSTALLATION IN VENICE

3 likes

Share

With the purpose of celebrating the opening of the first flagship of Louis Vuitton in Venice, the brand prepared an installation of art in the city.

Several shoes from the collection were lined around the city, scattered on historic monuments and by the famous gondolas, interacting as part of the scenery.
