The groom is the key “player”

Roberto Vicentti stands for the modern groom who wants to be a key “player” on his wedding day.

He wants to share with his future wife everybody’s attention. The day is unrepeatable and he has to make the most of it.

Results

The website was developed in order to get to know the history and values of Roberto Vicentti, its services and products/collections marketed. The image achieved in this project is the mirror of this brand, that is innovative, dynamic, professional, and with a strong sense of direction for the groom.
