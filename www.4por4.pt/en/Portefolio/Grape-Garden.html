Grape Garden

Website, Branding and Packaging

Explore

Portuguese soul in all its splendor

Grape Garden is a wine brand, located in Douro region, which sells its products in specialized stores.

Born in 2008, it was the result of the dream of young entrepreneurs who found in the wonderful region of Alto Douro an ideal place for the development of their enotourism project.

Results

The colors that were used represent feelings, environments, aromas and flavors that are typically hot and make us feel comfortable. ​The Portuguese soul, as well as the quality of the wine and the pleasure of drinking, is evident in the project developed.
