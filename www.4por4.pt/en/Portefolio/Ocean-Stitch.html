Ocean Stitch

Online Store, Branding and Packaging

Explore

Bold, trendy, fun and “cool” collections

In order to break with what is traditionally sold in mens clothing, in particular polos, t-shirts and long sleeves, arises Ocean Stitch - small details from Oporto...

They have bold, trendy, fun and “cool” collections, all of them designed with focus on “detail”, fitting and quality of raw materials (proudly made in Portugal).

Results

On this online store all products are properly structured into categories and collections, and for each we have the possibility of knowing its features, details and select the size and color we want.

Through the private area, each user can also access a clothing tracker. This tool allows an user, after finishing an order and receive an ID Number, to know the location of the people that also have a similar article.
