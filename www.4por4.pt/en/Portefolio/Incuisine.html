A KITCHEN TO YOUR NEEDS ...

This is the concept of this Swiss company, always looking for new trends, new materials and new solutions.    

In addition to the kitchens, this company also focuses on the commercialization of furniture for bathrooms and rooms.

Results

In Cuisine's commitment is to follow the customer from design to installation. It was also this assumption that we had, both in the development of the corporate image and later in the website.

 

Concerning to the logo, we focus on the letters - "IN" - making them in a wavy, readable and continuous lines. The typography used, in for the word 'Cuisine' after several experiences, is simultaneously contemporary, simple and harmonious.
