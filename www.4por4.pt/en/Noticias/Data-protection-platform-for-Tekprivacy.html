4por4 started the development of a platform for Tekprivacy

With the entry of the new GDPR, it´s estimated that about 28,000 DPOs are needed. TekPrivacy analyzed those numbers and asked 4por4 to develop a tool that would fit and meet all their needs.

The platform developed will allow:

• Characterization of personal data processing.
• Semi-automatic privacy impact assessment.
• Permanent update with new risks and vulnerabilities.
• Dashboard with the risk level of all institution's treatments.
• Reports with suggested measures to mitigate the risk of privacy.


We thanks for the opportunity it has given to the elaboration of this project with enormous complexity, but which will undoubtedly be a "milestone" in our history.
