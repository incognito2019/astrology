Climate Vulnerability in São Tomé e Príncipe

In the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe, the problem of global warming is not overlooked and has been one of the great priorities of the national authorities.

It was necessary to develop a set of communication supports in which, in a simple, innovative and functional way, could be possible to convey the existence of an institution created for this purpose.

Results

As part of an international public contest, in which 4por4 was the selected company, CNMC requested a strong image that would contribute to the promotion of true leadership of the CNMC in the different programs and projects developed with United Nations.

 

So, we developed a typographic logo, which is composed with three stripes of color, that represents the rainbow (referring to the climate) and at the same time the main colors of São Tomé´s flag.

 

Subsequently, all other media were developed, such as brochures, flyers, roll-ups, interior and exterior signage, billboards, powerpoint and word presentation templates, merchandising applications, etc.
