New Platform for Health Schools

Explore

Partilhar

4por4 in partnership with Vitae Professionals will start an innovative platform in Portugal

This plataform will host all the courses offers in the health area, more specifically related to the nursing schools. At a time of great demand for foreign students in our country to establish or start university studies, this platform will be the main tool to obtain all the information about the several schools, courses, cost of living in Portugal and in the cities where the schools are, among other features.

Each school will have its own micro-site that can be managed through its own private area.

4por4, thanks the opportunity given to develop this project, which will undoubtedly be a milestone in the dissemination of Portuguese education across borders.
