Toca a Rufar

Branding and Website W/ Responsive Design

Explore

Promotion of the portuguese drum...

Toca a Rufar is a project of artistic and cultural education, that promotes the Portuguese traditional percussion, which is distinguished by putting the Portuguese culture, knowledge and art, in a privileged position as a source of value, of development and contemporary.

Results

On this rebranding, the third since the beginning of our commercial relationship, we opted for developing an appealing graphic design, fresh and clean, with reference to elements linked to the sector in which the Band operates. Dynamism, interactivity and navigability, are three of the main points to remember.
