4por4 started the development of a B2B platform for Mondial Export

This international platform will allow any trader to place their products. After registering in a private area, the company can edit the profile and insert the services or products that want to sell.

All contacts and negotiation between companies will be carried out through this platform, which also counts with commissioners and partners for each country.

4por4, thanks the opportunity given to develop this project, which will undoubtedly be a "milestone" in our history.
