Your pharmacy at the sky resort…

For more than 50 years, the Pharmacie Internationale has been established in the center of Verbier a village, located in the commune of Bagnes, canton of Valais, in Switzerland.

Besides, having the best health advice, the customer can also find a selection of highly prestigious fragrances, beauty and make-up products.

Results

The aim of this project was achieved by the sophistication and sobriety implemented on the webdesign developed and also through the use of photos and graphic compositions.  

 

Dynamism and simplicity in access to information were two of the main points to be retained.
