40 years of quality and sophistication

Driven by a pioneering spirit since 1978, Castro Lighting's long history began with the desire to distribute lighting solutions around the world.

The passion for design naturally grew within, as inspiration came from exciting trips and personal experiences of Mr. Modesto Castro. It took numerous discoveries to assemble the necessary skills and courage to gain ground and create a brand with such a strong character as Castro Lighting.

Results

Delicate hand-wrought brass work, hammered or plain textures and polished or mate finishes are the key-points that make each lamp a true work of art created with no detail overlooked.

 

So, we developed a very attractive design, with a set of innovative, unique and distinct features that allow the visitor an excellent user experience.
